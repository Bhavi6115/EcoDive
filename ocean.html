Ocean Ecosystem

Oceans cover over 70% of Earth's surface and are crucial for climate regulation, oxygen production, and supporting marine biodiversity.

Challenges

  • Plastic pollution and marine debris
  • Overfishing and coral bleaching
  • Rising ocean temperatures and acidification

Solutions

  • Reducing plastic use and promoting recycling
  • Protecting marine habitats and sustainable fishing
  • Supporting marine conservation organizations
