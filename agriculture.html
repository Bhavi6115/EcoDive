Agricultural Ecosystem

Agricultural ecosystems are managed lands that provide food, fiber, and fuel. They play a crucial role in feeding the world but also face sustainability challenges.

Challenges

  • Soil degradation and erosion
  • Overuse of pesticides and fertilizers
  • Water scarcity and biodiversity loss

Solutions

  • Adopting sustainable farming techniques
  • Promoting crop rotation and organic farming
  • Implementing efficient irrigation systems
