Desert Ecosystem

Deserts are dry, arid regions that receive very little rainfall, often less than 25 cm annually. Despite harsh conditions, deserts support a variety of life forms adapted to extreme temperatures and limited water.

Challenges

  • Desertification due to overgrazing and deforestation
  • Water scarcity and overuse of underground aquifers
  • Climate change increasing desert expansion
  • Habitat loss affecting biodiversity

Solutions

  • Promote sustainable grazing and farming practices
  • Use drip irrigation and water conservation techniques
  • Plant drought-resistant vegetation to prevent erosion
  • Raise awareness and implement policies to reduce climate impact
