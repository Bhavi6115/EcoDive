Island Ecosystem

Islands are isolated habitats with high biodiversity and many endemic species. They are particularly vulnerable to climate change and invasive species.

Challenges

  • Sea-level rise threatening land and freshwater
  • Invasive species outcompeting native species
  • Limited resources and overdevelopment

Solutions

  • Promoting sustainable tourism and development
  • Controlling invasive species
  • Conservation education and island restoration
