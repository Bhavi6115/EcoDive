Polar Ecosystem

The polar regions—Arctic and Antarctic—are icy, remote, and vital to Earth's climate. They are home to unique wildlife and help regulate the planet's temperature by reflecting sunlight.

Challenges

  • Melting glaciers and ice caps
  • Loss of habitat for species like polar bears and penguins
  • Increased shipping and resource exploitation

Solutions

  • Reducing greenhouse gas emissions globally
  • Creating and enforcing protected areas
  • Supporting climate research and conservation efforts
