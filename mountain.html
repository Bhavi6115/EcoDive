Mountain Ecosystem

Mountains are important sources of freshwater and biodiversity. They host a variety of flora and fauna adapted to harsh climates and high altitudes.

Challenges

  • Glacier melting
  • Habitat loss due to tourism
  • Landslides and erosion

Solutions

  • Conservation of native species
  • Regulated tourism
  • Watershed management
